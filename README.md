# 🚀 CareerCompass

## **Kerala's AI-Powered Higher Education & Career Guidance Platform**

### 🎯 Vision
CareerCompass is a one-stop digital platform that helps students, parents, teachers, and career counsellors make informed higher education and career decisions through AI-powered guidance, personalized recommendations, interactive maps, and comprehensive educational resources.

## 🏆 Total Feature Count
CareerCompass includes **35+ major modules** with over **250 individual features**, making it a comprehensive, AI-powered digital ecosystem for higher education and career guidance. Rather than being just a college directory, it functions as a **personalized digital career mentor** that supports students from career exploration through college selection, admissions, scholarships, and future career planning. This combination of AI guidance, localized educational data, bilingual support, interactive maps, and responsive design makes CareerCompass a complete solution aligned with the hackathon problem statement.
